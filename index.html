Where a gift can go

Give generally, or direct your donation to one active program.

Water

Kajiado Borehole Fund

Drilling and solar-pumping for four villages currently trucking water in during dry months.

Nutrition

School Feeding, Ongata

Daily lunch for 640 pupils across three primary schools, sourced from local farmers.

Livelihood

Women's Irrigation Co-op

Drip-irrigation kits for a 40-member farming group, repaid into a community revolving fund.
