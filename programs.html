Programs

Three ways we show up, on repeat.

Msingi runs three standing programs rather than one-off campaigns. Each has its own budget, its own field officer, and its own line in the ledger — so you always know which door your gift walked through.

Stationery & Uniforms

School Essentials Fund

Every January and September, field officers visit partner primary schools to identify children turned away or shamed for missing uniforms or books. Each child receives a full uniform, shoes, and a term's stationery — sized and sourced from local tailors and stationers rather than shipped in bulk.

Nutrition

Widows' Nutrition Program

540 widows across Kajiado County receive a monthly food basket — maize, beans, cooking oil, and seasonal vegetables — delivered through a rotating network of community elders who visit each household in person. Elders also flag health concerns back to our team.

How your gift is spent

Boychild

Boychild Recovery & Mentorship

A two-stage program for young men working to overcome drug dependency: first, weekly counselling with a licensed addiction counsellor; then, once stabilised, placement with a mentor in a trade — welding, boda repair, or carpentry — so recovery has somewhere to go. Families are involved throughout.
